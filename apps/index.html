Apps

WristBoard

WristBoard (previously Shift Keyboard) is a full keyboard for Apple Watch. It supports multiple languages and QWERTY, QWERTZ and AZERTY keyboard layouts. There's also support for autocorrect, text suggestions and custom dictionaries too.

As featured on 9to5Mac, Cult of Mac, iMore, AppAdvice and iPhon.fr.

Technologies: SwiftUI, CoreData

TV Remote

TV Remote was created to replace an out-of-date app I was using for my TV and was heavily inspired by the Apple TV Remote app. It supports TVs from LG, Samsung, Sony, Roku and many more.

As featured on 9to5Mac (1), 9to5Mac (2), iPhon.fr and MacMagazine.

Technologies: SwiftUI, CoreData + CloudKit, SiriKit, WidgetKit

TV Launcher

TV Launcher was created as a way for UK Apple TV (and then iOS) users to quickly launch a live TV channel in its corresponding streaming app. Made for cord-cutters, TV Launcher has a convenient TV Guide too for being able to find something to watch live too.

As featured on iMore.

Technologies: SwiftUI, WidgetKit

HomeButtons

HomeButtons makes it easy to control Apple HomeKit Scenes and Accessories on Apple Watch or through the Widget.

Technologies: SwiftUI, CoreData + CloudKit, HomeKit, WidgetKit

Instructor

Instructor is an app designed for driving instructors that allows them to track their pupils’ progress in an easy to use app.

Technologies: SwiftUI, CoreData + CloudKit, StoreKit

Retro Keyboard

Retro Keyboard is T9 style keyboard for Apple Watch. Similar to old feature phones, you can use nine keys to type out messages.

Technologies: SwiftUI, CoreData

Haptics

Haptics is a tool specifically designed for iOS developers to allow them to test out the different types of haptic feedback that iOS has to offer. The Apple Watch companion app provides similar functionality for watchOS developers too.

Technologies: SwiftUI

MediWear

MediWear is medical ID app that has been designed around Apple Watch. Users enter their details on their iPhone and it will sync to their Apple Watch.

Technologies: UIKit, WatchKit, WatchConnectivity

UniClip

UniClip is a universal clipboard app. Users add some text or an image and the content will automatically sync to all of their other iCloud connected devices.

Technologies: UIKit, AppKit, CloudKit

WristChat

WristChat was an Apple Watch app for reading & responding to WhatsApp messages. It supports typing messages on a full keyboard via linking with WristBoard.

As featured on 9to5Mac, iMore, AppAdvice and Metro.

Technologies: SwiftUI

AllergyMe

AllergyMe was a medical ID app specifically designed for allergy sufferers. This was my very first iOS app and was created as I suffer from a peanut allergy.

As featured on BBC News, Anaphylaxis Campaign and Swindon Advertiser.

Technologies: UIKit, WatchKit, WatchConnectivity
