MediID Press Kit

MediID brings a full Medical ID to your iPhone, iPad & Apple Watch. If you ever get into a medical emergency, you can rely on MediID to be there to inform first responders about your medical details. Using the Widgets or Apple Watch app, responders can view your medical conditions, medication, general details and much more!
