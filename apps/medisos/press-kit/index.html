MediSOS Press Kit

MediSOS allows you to activate a medical emergency siren when needed most. MediSOS syncs with MediWidget and MediWear for a full medical ID experience.

Features

Medical Siren

Activate the MediSOS emergency siren if you find yourself in a medical emergency and need to attract the attention of people nearby so you can get help. The siren can be activated by pressing the big SOS button in the app or via the Widget and Apple Watch Complication. To prevent accidentally activating the alarm, there is a 5-second countdown before the siren activates. The siren can be stopped when viewing the medical ID when first responders get to you.

All medical ID data is stored securely on your own private iCloud account using the secure Keychain functionality. No data ever leaves your device.
