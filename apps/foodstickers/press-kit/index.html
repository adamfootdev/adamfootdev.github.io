Features

Stickers

Add fun food stickers to messages you send in iMessage! Choose one of the many stickers in the app and send it individually or attach it to an existing message. You can even use them in FaceTime too!

Pricing

Food Stickers is available as a one-time purchase for $0.99/£0.99. Promo codes available upon request.

Compatibility

Food Stickers is available on iOS (13.0+) and iPadOS (13.0+).

Technologies

Food Stickers has been built using Messages.

Localisation

Food Stickers is localised in English.
