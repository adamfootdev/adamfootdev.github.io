Bullseye

Apple Ads Manager

Bullseye makes it quick & easy to view & manage all of your Apple Ads campaigns from a native Mac app. With Bullseye, you will no longer need to login to the Apple Ads website as all of your data is ready to view as soon as you open the app. By having a native Mac app, you'll be able to take advantage of keyboard shortcuts, toolbar customisation and system features like dark mode. Bullseye is available to download on macOS.

Features

Manage Campaigns

Easily manage your Apple Ads campaigns from one place with the ability to make changes or drill down into ad groups and keywords.

Target New Keywords

Add broad & exact keywords to an ad group and set a max bid or block negative terms you no longer want to bid on.

Activate, Pause & Remove

Quickly activate, pause or remove campaigns, ad groups or keywords to ensure you are getting the best value for money.

Manage Targeting

Set a schedule when ads should run, which audiences to target and much more.

Budgets, Bids & Caps

Control your monthly spending with budgets, bids & caps to suit your budget.

Multiple Accounts

No need to keep logging in with the ability to compare data across multiple Apple Ads accounts.

Multiple Windows

Quickly compare data across multiple campaigns side-by-side with support for multiple windows.

Dark Mode

No more blinding white light when working late.

Technologies

Bullseye has been built using SwiftUI, Keychain, StoreKit and TipKit.
