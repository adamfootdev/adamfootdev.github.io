MediWear Press Kit

MediWear is a medical ID app designed specifically for Apple Watch. MediWear syncs with MediWidget and MediSOS for a full medical ID experience.

Features

Apple Watch Medical ID

MediWear has been designed for your Apple Watch so all of your medical details are within easy reach. You’ll be able to view personal details, medical conditions, allergies & reactions, medication and emergency contacts - all on your wrist. Access all of this without the need to unlock your iPhone!

Complications

There’s no need to even launch the app as all of your medical details can be added to your Watch Face through the MediWear Complication. This is perfect for displaying to first responders who can then tap it and view even more detail about you.

Emergency Contacts

If you’ve chosen to add emergency contacts to the app, you’ll be able to call them with just a tap from the app or Complication.

Customisation

MediWear offers the ability to fully customise the Complication and whether a Star of Life or medical Cross is used when using the app launcher Complication.

Translate

Translate medical conditions, medications and allergies/reactions when travelling abroad.

Stored Securely

All medical ID data is stored securely on your own private iCloud account using the secure Keychain functionality. No data ever leaves your device.

Pricing

MediWear is available as a one-time purchase for $2.99/£2.99. Promo codes available upon request.

Compatibility

MediWear is available on iOS (17.0+) and watchOS (10.0+).

Technologies

MediWear has been built using SwiftUI, App Intents, Keychain, Translation and WidgetKit.

Localisation

MediWear is localised in English.
