NQSubBlock Press Kit

NQSubBlock is a Safari Content Blocker used for Newsquest websites in the UK. Newsquest own many local newspapers and as such often block important local stories with a subscription popup that cannot be removed unless subscribed. This app removes it.

To find out whatʼs new in NQSubBlock, please head to the releases page here.

Features

Unlock Articles

Get back to reading your local news without being prompted to subscribe when you have only read a few articles.

Pricing

NQSubBlock is available as a one-time purchase for $0.99/£0.99. Promo codes available upon request.

Compatibility

NQSubBlock is available on iOS (16.0+), iPadOS (16.0+), macOS (13.0+) and visionOS (1.0+).

Technologies

NQSubBlock has been built using SwiftUI.

Localisation

NQSubBlock is localised in English.
