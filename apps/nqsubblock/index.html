NQSubBlock

Block Newsquest Subscription Banners

NQSubBlock is a Safari Content Blocker used for Newsquest websites in the UK. Newsquest own many local newspapers and as such often block important local stories with a subscription popup that cannot be removed unless subscribed. This app removes it. NQSubBlock is available to download on iOS, iPadOS, macOS and visionOS.

Features

Unlock Articles

Get back to reading your local news without being prompted to subscribe when you have only read a few articles.

Technologies

NQSubBlock has been built using SwiftUI.
