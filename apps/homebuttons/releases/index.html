HomeButtons Releases

Version 2.3

9 May 2025

CarPlay

Open doors, turn on lights or set a scene when arriving home with the new HomeButtons for CarPlay app. Set accessories & scenes in Settings > CarPlay on your iPhone.

Version 2.1

16 September 2024

Fall 2024 Update

• Added dark & tinted app icons on iOS & iPadOS. • Added support for the latest version of iOS, iPadOS, macOS & watchOS.

Version 2.0

10 May 2024

Redesigned App

HomeButtons has been fully redesigned with the ability to control scenes & accessories from within the main app on iPhone, iPad and Mac. HomeButtons makes it quick & easy to toggle accessory states or activate scenes.

New Widgets

Widgets have been completely redesigned and now offer the ability to choose multiple accessories/scenes on a single widget. They are also now fully interactive so controls can be used without the need for the app to open. A selection of Lock Screen widgets are also available too.

Updated Apple Watch App

The Apple Watch app has been redesigned to make it quicker to select accessories & scenes.

iCloud Sync

Chosen accessories & scenes now sync over iCloud so you'll see the same list across all of your iCloud connected devices.
